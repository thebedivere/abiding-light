Statement of Faith

  1. We believe in one God who is eternally existent in three Persons – The Father, Son, and Holy Ghost.
  2. We believe that the Bible is the infallible and preserved Word of God revealed to men.
  3. We believe that all have sinned and have fallen short of the glory of God and that acceptance of Christ is necessary for salvation.
  4. We believe in Tithing.
  5. We believe in the Great Commission.
  6. We believe that the Christian is to strive to live a holy life before God.
  7. We believe in the Special Creation of this earth and all life as recorded in the Book of Genesis.
  8. We believe in Water Baptism by immersion in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Ghost.
  9. We believe that Water Baptism is an outward symbol of the inward work that has been performed by Christ.
  10. We believe in the Lord’s Supper and that is to be observed in remembrance of Christ until He comes.
  11. We believe in the Baptism of the Holy Ghost with the initial evidence of speaking in other tongues.
  12. We believe in the Gifts of the Spirit.
  13. We believe in Divine Healing.
  14. We believe in the reward of eternal life for the redeemed and the eternal punishment of the wicked in hell.
  15. We believe in the Second Coming of Christ.
